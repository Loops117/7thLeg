About Us

What started as a simple shop for insect-keeping supplies has grown into something much bigger. When I launched this site, my main goal was to share the products I use and trust. But as I looked around the hobby, I noticed a problem: there wasn’t a central place for keepers to track their species lists and easily share that information with others.

So I built one. Today, our site is more than just a storefront — it’s a place for invert keepers to connect, learn, and share.

How This Works

  • Track your collections and wish lists
  • Create and share a shareable species list
  • Browse and add photos & care knowledge
  • Join a network of passionate keepers
