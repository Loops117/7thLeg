The Startup

7th Leg began as a small shop for insect-keeping supplies but quickly evolved into the first peer-driven social platform for invert enthusiasts. We created a central, trusted space where keepers can track collections, connect, exchange knowledge, and join a transparent marketplace.

Built on community accountability, 7th Leg uses peer reviews, badges, and store/buyer ratings to ensure trust and reliability across the platform.
