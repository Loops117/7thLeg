About Us

What started as a simple shop for insect-keeping supplies has grown into something much bigger. We noticed there wasn’t a central place for keepers to track species and connect. So we built one.

Today, 7th Leg is more than a storefront — it’s a place for invert keepers to connect, learn, and share.
